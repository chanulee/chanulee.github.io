About

Chanwoo Lee is an HCI design engineer based in London, UK and Seoul, South Korea.

He defines himself as an architect of the information age. He tries to define a co-living relationship among human, data and nature(environment) by exploring and designing computer interaction, interface and system.

His aim is to design harmonic computer that is not dominant, which is fluid and context-aware. To achieve this, his research area lies in intelligent and spatial computing.

He is currently pursuing his masters degree (MA/MSc) in Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and Royal College of Art.
