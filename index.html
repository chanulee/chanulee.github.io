CHANWOO LEE

Computer Architect
Human and Data

Chanwoo Lee is an HCI researcher, design engineer and media artist based in London and Seoul.

He designs and develops computer interaction, interface and system for human creativity & productivity. Defining relationship between human and data, he designs information environment.

He is currently pursuing MA/MSc in Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and Royal College of Art.

His research interest lies in human-computer interaction, intelligent and spatial computing. Interaction-centric AI, Social and Interactive AI, fluid interface, context-aware, calm technology, semantic file system, graph and machine learning.

Sketch Rotom

Liberating sketch experience with multi-modal genAI for artists and designers

Sketching is an essential tool for artists and designers to brainstorm, visualise and communicate the idea. They often use hands or talk to themselves while sketching, and it helps to think and imagine. What don't we utilise that for more creative sketching?

Sketch Rotom, named after pokemon, is a concept to augment human creativity by assisting sketch and streamlining visualizing with multi-modal genAI.

Prototyping is on progress using openFrameworks, Wekinator and RunwayML.
