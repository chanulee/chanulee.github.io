Manifesto

Back to human.

Computer designers and engineers are the new architect of the information age.

Data become information when meeting human,
and we design that relationship around computers.

Architects have been designing and engineering environments
and ‘machines that we live in’ along the human history,
and now we’re (re)joining them.

Computer is a machine that we live in and with.
It is an entity itself and an environment at the same time.

It serves human and affects human -

human behaviour,
thinking,
cognition,
sense,
habits,
relationships,
playing,
learning,
working,
loving,
and living.

We were just driving a car with max speed, optimisation in driver’s seat.
And making better things don’t always making things better.
We must rethink humanism in the information age.

What kind of life do we want?
How can we make it happen with computers?
