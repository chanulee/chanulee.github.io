Computer Architect Manifesto

Computer designers and engineers are the new architects of the information age.


Architects have designed and engineered ‘machines for living in’ throughout human history.


Today, as computer architects, we redefine environment to also encompass virtual and cognitive spaces.


Computer is a machine that we live in and with.


Data becomes information when interpreted by humans,

and we design that relationship with computers.


It serves and affects human -


human behaviour,

thinking,

cognition,

sense,

habits,

relationships,

playing,

learning,

working,

loving,

and living.


We must reconsider humanism in the information age, in the era of relentless technology advancement.


We must codesign technologies and behaviours to build the future.


We stand at a pivotal moment:

What kind of life do humans envision?

How can we make it happen with computers?
