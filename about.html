CHAN WOO LEE (HE/HIM)

Chanwoo is a problem solver - designer, engineer - and problem seeker - artist. - with a keen interest in contemporary and emerging technologies such as Web, AI, XR, Robotics and its interaction with human and society.

He is a research intern at MALer Lab, Sogang University and has been running a multi-disciplinary design studio Superbloom since 2019.

Chanwoo's works have been presented at various conferences and exhibitions including HCI Korea ('19 '21), Korea Computer Congress'22, Festival of Nature ('22, UK). Some of his clients include Art Center Nabi, MBC, and KATP.
