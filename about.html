ABOUT

Chanwoo (Chanu) Lee
  • he/him
  • AI/HCI Research Engineer
  • Currently: London, UK

Chanwoo is an AI/HCI Research Engineer passionate about designing flexible and personalized computer - using intelligent & spatial computing.

He designs and develops computer interface and system with growing expertise in AI and XR technology. His aim is to contribute to the innovation of intelligent and spatial computing to create more personal, context-aware and fluid computer system.

He is pursuing Master of Science and Master of Arts in Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and Royal College of Art.
