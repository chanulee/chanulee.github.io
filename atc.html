ATC21
Quantum Jump: Leap into the Future

About

At Art & Technology Conference 2021, we predict, imagine, and express the life of our humanity that will be changed by digital, and draw on the future through discussions on its direction.
We present the Layered Reality, Convivial System, and Resonance as sub-themes as keywords that members of art & technology are currently contemplating in the field of digital creativity. Meet projects and events that contain reality technology and the questions triggered by it, human and machine, human and environment, and material interactions, and movements in our society.
