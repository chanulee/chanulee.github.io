About

Chanwoo Lee FRSA (he/him) is an Imperial-and-RCA-trained design engineer, researcher and media artist. Designing Computational Media

Co-designing technology and behaviour to build the future. Just like an architect: He designs information technology and human relationship of the future.

He is pursuing MA/MSc Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and the Royal College of Art. Prior to his masters, he received his BAS in Art & Technology, and BBA in Technology, Society and Entrepreneurship from Sogang University, Seoul.
