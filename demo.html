About

Chanwoo Lee is an HCI design engineer based in Seoul, South Korea and London, UK.

He sees himself as an architect of information society. He tries to redefine the relationship between human, data and nature by exploring and designing computer interaction, interface and system.

His aim is to design 'renaissance' computer, which is fluid, personalized and context-aware. To achieve this, his research area lies in intelligent and spatial computing.

He is currently pursuing his masters degree (MA/MSc) in Innovation Design Engineering at Imperial College London and Royal College of Art.
