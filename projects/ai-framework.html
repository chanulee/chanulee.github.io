Design Framework for Responsible AI

Imperial Dyson School of Design Engineering · ICML 2024 London

Responsible AI (RAI) is dealt with more on the research and academic side rather than the industry side. There's a gap between RAI research and product development. A framework that can embed RAI in the product design process is needed to bridge this gap.

The requirements of this framework include: • Exchange of knowledge to understand both human behaviour and technology • Space to think creatively about the impact and specifics of AI and human • Intermittent evaluation of impact in 'Responsible ideation' and prototyping
