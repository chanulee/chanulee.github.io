human.intheloop

Nabi AI Open Lab

A new workforce performing human intelligence tasks, such as data labeling and annotation, has emerged in the fourth industrial revolution. Their efforts contribute to further refining artificial intelligence models designed to replace human labor.

Our work intends to provide an interactive and gamified experience for the audience to perform such labor firsthand and visualize the compensations in return. The mundane yet incessant nature of the tasks reminds of the extinct occupations in the previous industrial revolutions, like assembly line workers, telephone operators, and human computers.
