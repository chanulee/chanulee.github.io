Sunny Day

Lifestyle Media Group · IEEE VR 2024

Interactive MR system for immersive storytelling capable of high degree of freedom in creative expressions, integrating UWB sensors, Azure Kinect and htc Vive.

Solarpunk media art about a future where pixels approach the realm of atom, and the climate crisis has been overcome.

As a research team lead, I led research, design & engineering of the MR system and developed wearable input device.
As a co-artist, we worked on research for future scenario and current humanity's climate technologies.
