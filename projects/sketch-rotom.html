Sketch Rotom

Chelsea Interactive

Liberating sketch experience with multi-modal genAI for artists and designers. Sketching is an essential tool for artists and designers to brainstorm, visualize, and communicate their ideas. They often use their hands or talk to themselves while sketching, which helps them think and imagine. Why don't we utilize this for more creative sketching?

Sketch Rotom, named after the Pokémon Rotom, is a concept designed to augment human creativity by assisting with sketches and streamlining visualization through multi-modal generative AI.

Prototyping is in progress using openFrameworks, Wekinator and RunwayML.
