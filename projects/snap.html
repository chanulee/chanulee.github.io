Sensory Misalignment

Snap Inc.

Sensory misalignment is a research and interactive experience questioning the reality.

Reality is actually a processed electronic signal in our brain.
Then, what is an actual reality and what is an augmented one?

To genuinely see and experience the world, we need to break away from our automatic perception, aiming to disrupt the automatic and unconscious recognition processes by creating sensory mismatches that challenge users' expectations.
