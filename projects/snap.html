Sensory Misalignment

Snap Inc.

Shared playful experience of non-verbal communication with human body on Spectacles and WebAR

Display, whether it's transparent or not, right in front of your eyes might be quite lonely. There's some subtle information and message in non-verbal communication and it's often lost within AR interaction. Using non-linear gamified storytelling and body movement leveraging Connected Lenses, and Hand Tracking.
